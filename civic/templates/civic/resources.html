More Ways to Get Involved!

In order to be the most active and involved as you can, here's some additional resources to help! This page contains a list of petitions, articles, and information on other organizations.

Note that these links will take you to external sites, not owned or operated by Civic Connect or The Full Stack Citizens.

Look at Change.org for a list of good petitions to sign, and to see how many people are supporting these causes. Also see the organizations below:

  • Coalition for Rainforest Nations
  • Clean Air Task Force
  • Rainforest Foundation US

The Washington Post has good resources to educate and understand the long history of systemic racism in the United States. Look at Change.org for a list of good petitions to sign, and to see how many people are supporting these causes. Here are some good additional organizations to get involved with:

  • Color of Change
  • National Association for the Advancement of Colored People
  • Black Lives Matter

The New York Times also has a good section on articles regarding gun reform. See Change.org for a petition calling for common sense gun laws. The organization Moms Demand Action for Gun Sense in America is a grass-roots organization of Moms demanding action following the Sandy Hook Elementary School shooting in 2012. Another good organization is the Brady Campaign to Prevent Gun Violence works to pass and enforce public policy, laws and legislation through grass-roots activism. Also see the organizations below:

  • Coalition to Stop Gun Violence
  • Everytown for Gun Safety
  • Gun Free Kids

The New York Times has good articles on healthcare reform in the United States. Look at Change.org for a list of good petitions to sign, and to see how many people are supporting these causes. Here are some good additional organizations to get involved with:

  • National Coalition on Health Care
  • American Nurses Association
  • Families USA

Currently, the United States has over 1.7 trillion dollars held in student debt. The New York Times has good articles on the student debt crisis in the United States. Look at Change.org for a list of good petitions to sign, and to see how many people are supporting this cause. Here are some good additional organizations to get involved with:

  • Student Debt Crisis
  • American Federation of Teachers
  • Reclaim the American Dream

Over 400,000 people have died in the United States over the last two decades due to opioid overdoes. The Washington Post has good articles on this epidemic, and follows its history back to the 1990s. Look at Change.org for a list of good petitions to sign, and to see how many people are supporting this cause. These organizations are fighting to make a change:

  • The National Academy of Medicine
  • Federal Office of Rural Health Policy
  • Agency for Healthcare Research and Quality

The New York Times has good articles on the vaccination controversy in the United States. Look at Change.org for a list of good petitions to sign, and to see how many people are supporting this cause. Here are some good additional organizations to get involved with:

  • Vaccinate Your Family
  • Voices for Vaccines
  • Gavi
